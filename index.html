Hockey Training

Hockey requires a mix of on-ice and off-ice training to build the skills, speed, and strength needed to excel.

Private Lessons

Private hockey lessons allow for personalized coaching that accelerates skill development. One-on-one training lets coaches evaluate a player's abilities and tailor drills to their specific needs.

Ski Touring

Hockey ski touring combines cross-country skiing with the puck handling skills of hockey for a unique outdoor experience.

Booking

Booking ice time and facilities is an essential part of hockey operations. Hockey programs must coordinate scheduling based on team numbers, skill levels, and availability of coaches.

Pricing

The costs associated with hockey can add up quickly for players and families. At the recreational level, there are registration fees to join a league or team, which may range from $100-300 per season.
