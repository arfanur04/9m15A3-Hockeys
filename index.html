Program Sections

This sports development plan outlines strategies and objectives to grow participation and improve performance across junior and senior hockey teams.

Junior Program

Improve junior team performance with 50% of teams reaching finals by Year 3. An annual review will measure participation rates, team performance and player retention.

Teenager Program

Promote youth hockey through school visits, social media, and by distributing flyers. Develop relationships with local schools and physical education teachers to promote hockey

Professional Program

Recruitment of high caliber coaches with professional playing experience Sign sponsorship deals to increase funding for talent scouting and facilities Install sports science monitoring to optimize player conditioning and prevent injuries
